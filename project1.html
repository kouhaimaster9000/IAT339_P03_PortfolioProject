Visual Novel Project

A Sight Never Seen

For IAT313: Narrative and New Media, we were tasked with creating an interactive story that has more than one linear plotline and multiple endings. We had to create this project in a group of four, and develop and establish a narrative that the player could control with the choices given to them like a ‘choose your adventure’ story. The approach to the project was flexible. However the main outcome was to create a non-linear story.
