About Me

Hello! My name is Crystal and I am a third year student who is studying Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University! My strengths lie in illustration, design, and a little bit of coding.

My goal is to be able to create beautiful designs that are touching to people, to create a new experience for them. Throughout my education, I have collaborated and worked with many people in projects that includes collaborative writing, web design, film and game design.

Outside of design work, I enjoy video games, making stories, reading, and learning new languages. I hope to be able to inspire many people, as well as to inspire myself through new experiences!
