About Me

Hello! My name is Crystal and I am a third year student who is studying Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University! My strengths lie in illustration and design.

I enjoy drawing 2D character illustrations, as well as designing characters and interfaces that are simple and clean. My hope is to create illustrations that will inspire hope in others and encourage them to start creating as well. I am proficient in many digital illustration softwares such as Adobe Suite. Clip Studio Paint, Procreate and Paint Tool Sai. I am always open to learning and the oppourtunity to be able to create!

Tools:

  • Wacom Bamboo Splash Tablet
  • Clip Studio Paint, Procreate, Paint Tool Sai
  • Adobe Suite
